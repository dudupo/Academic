Let $\ket{f}$ be a codeword in $C_{X}$, and let $X_{g}$ be the indicator that equals $1$ if $f$ has support on $X_{g}$, and $0$ otherwise. Observes that applying $T^{\otimes}$ on $\ket{f}$ yilds the state: 
\begin{equation*}
  \begin{split}
    T^{\otimes n}\ket{f} & =  T^{\otimes n}\ket{\sum_{g} X_{g}g } = \exp \left( i\pi/4 \sum_{g} X_{g}|g|  -  2 \cdot \pi/4 \sum_{g,h} X_{g}X_{h}|g\cdot h| \\
    & +  4 \cdot i\pi/4 \sum_{g,h} X_{g}X_{h}X_{l}|g\cdot h \cdot l| -   8  \cdot i\pi/4 \cdot \text{ integers } \right)\ket{f}
  \end{split}
\end{equation*}
